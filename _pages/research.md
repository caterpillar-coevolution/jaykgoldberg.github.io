We study the evolution of plant-insect interactions from both ecological and molecular/genetic perspectives. Our primary study systems are interactions between native Solanaceous plants (e.g. Datura spp., wild tobaccos) and their specialist herbivores in the Southwestern United States; however, we have many ongoing projects with other systems as well.
