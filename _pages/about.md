The Coevolutionary Genetics Lab at is a part of the School of Life Sciences at Arizona State Unviersity and led by Dr. Jay Goldberg. We use an interdisciplinary approach to explore the evolutionary processes governing species interactions from both ecological and molecular perspectives. Our research focuses on interactions between toxic native plants in the Sonoran Desert - such as western jimsonweed (Datura wrightii) - and the handful of specialist herbivores that thrive on chemically defended hosts.
